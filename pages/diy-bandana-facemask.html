DIY Bandana Face Mask
Very Easy / No Sew Mask

As the Covid-19 outbreak numbers continue to rise, we have been staying closely informed with all the essential information from our government, local government and the CDC.

Today, we learned of an update from the CDC where they are considering DIY masks for personal use, especially when running essential errands during this time.  To help out, we created an easy way to make a DIY Mask at home with any bandana you have and elastic ties (or rubber bands).  Shop bandanas at the bottom of the article for 40% off.

Article from NY Times // It's Time To Make Your Own Face Mask 

It shouldnt have come to this, but here we are. The world is running out of face masks for health care workers, which is one reason American officials, including the surgeon general, have warned members of the public against buying their own masks for protection against the coronavirus.

But that doesnt mean face masks for the public are a bad idea, if we had enough masks. Contrary to what American officials told us, many studies show that widespread mask-wearing might be a very effective complement to hand-washing, social-distancing and other measures to mitigate the pandemic. Health officials in China, Hong Kong, Singapore and Japan suggest that people wear masks in certain situations if theyre symptomatic, for instance, or if theyre in crowded, not-very-well-ventilated places, like airplanes. Studies have also shown that mask-wearing (in conjunction with hand-washing) reduces the spread of infection within households or other shared living spaces, like residence halls.

But how to get your hands on a mask, when there are no masks? The internet has a plan: Make your own. (full article)

Or Use A Step By Step Tutorial

Materials Needed

One Bandana
Two Hair Ties (Or Rubber Bands)

Materials Above 

Steps:

Step One:
Layout your bandana flat

Step Two:
Fold in Half Once, downwardFold in Half downward again
Flatten with your hands

Step Three:
Add a hair tie to either side of your bandana (about 1/4 of the way on either edge)

Step Four:
Fold both ends toward the center.

Step Five:
Tuck the inside of the corners into each other, just play around with it until it is secure.

Step Six:
Pull the elastic out

All finished! 
You can use the hair ties to secure the bandana around your face.

Easy to Wear and will stay secure for essential outings.
